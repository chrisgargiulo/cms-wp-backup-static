Take Care of Yourself<\/h2>\n\n\n\n
  • Get Healthy<\/strong>. Establish a healthy personal routine that includes exercise, eating healthy, and getting plenty of sleep regularly. Start by being mindful and honest with yourself and set realistic goals.<\/li>
  • Get Organized<\/strong>. Clean up your workspace and computer. Start with your desktop and go through all of your old project folders and files. Establish a healthy digital routine that includes backing up your data (e.g. in the cloud) regularly.<\/li>
  • Get Ahead<\/strong>. Do not wait for the next project or deadline. You can always work on internal documentation such as your resume, portfolio, and personal design system and branding assets.<\/li>
  • Get Educated<\/strong>. There is so much to learn and so many wonderful books, websites, and educational resources available. Start by drafting a summer reading list and then stick to it. Consider taking an online course and\/or completing online tutorials.<\/li><\/ul>\n\n\n\n
