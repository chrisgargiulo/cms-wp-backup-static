Showcase select projects by coding an online portfolio site from scratch<\/h3>\n\n\n\n

There are many different ways to share your work online. If you are a web designer with front-end web development skills looking to showcase your talent, one of the best ways is to custom design and code your own portfolio site from scratch. In this exercise, we will create a portfolio with three “featured” items. This “depth-over breadth” approach is well suited for designers that want to showcase a smaller amount of projects and go into great detail for each one. Case studies are well suited to tell a story and highlight the design process. <\/p>\n\n\n\n
